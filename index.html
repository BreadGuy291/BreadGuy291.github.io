Top 10 of The Best Roblox Games to Play

Written By Bread.

We all know that there are millions of games out in Roblox. Some of them being simple games to fun and complex ones.

Here are the Top 10 Best Roblox Games in my opinion.

1. Pet Simulator 99

Pet Simulator 99 is the new game out of the Pet Simulator Franchise. It's a game about mining coins with your pets and using those coins to hatch more eggs and unlock different areas.

It might seem simple, but, there is also other things in the game that you can do like:

Going to the Trading Plaza.

Getting Enchants and Potions.

Get Achievements.

2. Blade Ball

Blade ball is a fun game where you colelct different sword skins and abilities to compete for the number one spot.

It's all about timing your hits on the ball. This could take a while to master, but it will ensure you always get some kills in the hit game.

3. The Strongest Battlegrounds

The Strongest Battlegrounds is inspired by the hit anime, One Punch Man.

In the game, you play as different characters such as The Strongest Hero, Hero Hunter, etc.

In the game, you run around and try to kill people in the game using your different abilities. It might take some training, but it will definently pay off.

4. Blox Fruits

Blox Fruits is yet another game that is inspired by an anime, One Piece.

In the game, you can play as a Marine or a Pirate. You do quests in the game and earn levels to unlock different islands. You can also get different abilities like Fruits, Combat Styles, Swords, and Guns.

If you level up enough you can eventually reach The Second Sea and The Third Sea.
